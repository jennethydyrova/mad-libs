The Grinch is a(n) happy[a] green[a] creature with green[a] eyes who does not like Christmas cheer. When he sees people celebeating Christmas, it makes him angry[a]. He and his[a] Max try to spoil[v] Christmas for people in the town of People[n]-ville by stealing[v] their Christmas presents[n]. When people still have Christmas cheer, even without presents[n], Grinch decides to return the presents[n] and celebrates Christmas after all
